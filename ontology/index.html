Attributes

Attributes are matters-of-fact with regard to the state (i.e. the history) of the Ethereum Blockchain.

Mechanisms

Mechanisms are parts of the blockchain whose operation results on the commission of Attributes to the state.

Assignees

Assignees are the operators and protocol which bring about the functioning of Mechanisms in line with the previously given attributes.

Ethereology is an experiential resource for learning about Ethereum.
